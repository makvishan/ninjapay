Pay Ninja UPI Collect

Empower Your Business with seamless UPI Payments.

UPI opens up new opportunities for MSMEs, startups, corporates, NBFCs, and MFIs to accept payments from various sources directly into their bank accounts by creating a Virtual Private Address (VPA).

Ease of Use:

UPI payments are highly regarded for their simplicity. By setting up a VPA, businesses provide an easy and efficient way for customers to transfer funds directly into their corporate accounts.

Flexibility:

Accepting UPI payments allows businesses to receive money from multiple sources, such as individuals and other businesses, thus enhancing the range of payment options available.

Security:

UPI transactions are backed by h5 security measures, ensuring that both the payer and the payee can complete the payment process with confidence and safety.

Lower Transaction Fees:

UPI payments generally have lower fees compared to traditional payment methods, depending on the pricing structure, offering potential cost savings for businesses.

Faster Settlements:

UPI transactions are known for their quick settlement times, allowing businesses to access their funds more quickly than with many other payment methods.

Types of QR

Static QR Code

A static QR code can accept payments of the amount as displayed by NPCI and is open to the customer to enter the payment amount.

Dynamic QR Code

Dynamic QR code is meant for one-time use and has a fixed amount assigned to it, allowing the customer to pay only the specified amount linked to the QR code.

UPI Intent

In the intent flow (meant for mobile apps), when the customer selects the UPI payment app during checkout, the app is automatically launched on their mobile device.
