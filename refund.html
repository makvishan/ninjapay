Refund and Cancellation Policy

Effective Date: November 6, 2024

Rectangle Fintech Pvt Ltd ("we", "us", or "Payninja" ) provides a user-friendly interface for digital financial services in partnership with RBI licensed banks. This Refund and Cancellation Policy outlines circumstances under which a refund may be issued for payments made through TrustXpay.

1. Circumstances for Refund

We will generally not process refunds for payments made through the payment gateway. However, exceptions exist in the following situations:

  • Technical Error: Multiple debits from your card/bank account or excess debit in a single transaction due to a technical error. In such cases, the excess amount excluding payment gateway charges will be refunded.
  • Unsuccessful Enrollment: Payment charged but enrollment for a service is unsuccessful due to a technical error. We will prioritize resolving the enrollment issue at no additional cost. However, if you prefer a refund, you will receive the net amount after deduction of payment gateway charges or any other charges.

  • Unforeseeable circumstances beyond our control (e.g., fire, natural disasters, bank strikes, power failures, system failures).
