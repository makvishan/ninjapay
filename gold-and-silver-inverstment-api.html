How does the Digital Gold & Silver API work?

Ninja Pay's MMTC Digital Gold & Silver API streamlines the gold and silver investment process. Customers register on the platform, providing required information.

They enter their desired investment amount, authenticate the transaction with a PIN, and generate a Buy or Sell quotation using the API.

Before proceeding with payment, customers review and validate the quotation. The API ensures secure and seamless transaction execution.

Notably, purchased gold and silver are issued digitally to customers, eliminating the need for physical safekeeping.

Customers conveniently monitor real-time market prices, manage holdings, and benefit from gold and silver inflation hedge and portfolio diversification advantages. The API provides a user-friendly interface, secure transactions, and peace of mind for investors.
